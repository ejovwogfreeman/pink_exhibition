About Pink Exhibition

What We Are

Pink Exhibition is the biggest tradefair in Ebonyi state, aim is to help other vendors to connect, collaborate, and have the visibility to enable visitors shop at discount prizes.

This event has been successfully done for 4 years and in 2024 hosted over 50 vendors.

The event for 2024 was remarkable not just that it exceeded expectations but was graced by the Executive Governor of Ebonyi state and the first lady.

What We Do

Pink Exhibition is not just helping vendors but also business owners and brands to have the right platform they need to showcase, sell and succeed.

At Pink Exhibition, it is easy to recognize the achievement, resilience and commitment of top brands & vendors community as the heart of its culture every year.

At Pink Exhibition, the biggest tradefair in Ebonyi, Abakaliki, every vendor has the opportunity to be seen by over 10,000+ ready to buy audience. BE PART OF EBONYI'S BIGGEST TRADE EXHIBITION

MARYANN OMEGE (MIMS)

Convener, Pink Exhibition

Maryann Chinelo Omege, popularly known as Mims, is a dynamic entrepreneur, business strategist, and the convenor of the biggest trade fair in Ebonyi State – The Pink Exhibition. With a vision to create a thriving marketplace for businesses, Mims has successfully built the Pink Exhibition into a powerful business hub. At last year’s edition, over 7,000 people shopped from 68 vendors, making it the most impactful trade fair in the state.

Beyond the exhibition, Mims is a serial entrepreneur and the founder of multiple thriving brands, including:

Mims Beautilocks – A fast-growing haircare brand

Mims Logistics – A reliable delivery service

NC3 Brand – A business branding company

Bellz Noodles & Fills Food – Food brands serving high-quality local snacks

Pink Exhibition – The largest trade fair in Ebonyi State

Maryann Omege Foundation – An NGO dedicated to community impact

Mims is also a brand builder passionate about helping businesses grow, scale, and dominate their industry. She has mentored and coached countless entrepreneurs, helping them build structured and profitable brands.
