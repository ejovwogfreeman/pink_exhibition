Rules and Regulations for Vendors

  1. Category Selection: Vendors must choose one product or service category during registration and cannot sell outside their approved category. Violations may result in fines or removal.
  2. Limited Slots: Each category has a limited number of vendor slots to ensure fair competition. Early registration is recommended.
  3. Booth Representation: Booths and marketing materials must reflect the registered category for easy identification by attendees.
  4. Payment Terms: Only two installment payments are allowed, and full payment must be completed within the registration period.
  5. Equipment & Materials: Vendors must provide or rent their own equipment and materials.
  6. Banner Restriction: Standing banners are prohibited; instead, vendors should use bold banners attached to the top of their booths.
  7. Promotion & Publicity: Vendors must participate in promotional activities, including social media campaigns, for at least a month before the fair.
  8. Subletting Ban: Vendors cannot share or rent out their stalls to unregistered vendors.
  9. No Refunds: Ensure availability before registering, as refunds are not provided.
  10. Product Quality & Pricing: Businesses must sell quality items and offer competitive discounts below market prices.
